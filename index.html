Qian Sun is currently an associate professor at College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University (TJU) . She worked in Fraunhofer Singapore as a research fellow before joining TJU. Qian received her PhD degree from Nanyang Technological University. Qian’s research interests include Computer Graphics (CG), Human Computer Interaction (HCI), Machine Learning (ML) and Data Visualization. She has published around 15 papers on top-tier international conference and journals, including TOG, TVCG, CHI and so on.
